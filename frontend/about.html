Our Mission: Safer Roads for All.

RoadFix is a community-driven platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens and local authorities to ensure our infrastructure stays in top shape.

Why RoadFix?

Poor road conditions like potholes, broken streetlights, and faded signs aren't just inconvenient—they're dangerous. Traditionally, reporting these issues involved complex bureaucracy and long wait times.

RoadFix changes that by providing a streamlined, GPS-enabled reporting tool that puts the power of change directly into the hands of the citizens.

Start Reporting

Precision Tracking

Using real-time geolocation, we pinpoint exactly where the issue is, making it easier for repair crews to find and fix it.

Public Accountability

Our public dashboard allows everyone to see reported issues and track their progress from "Reported" to "Resolved".

Faster Response

By digitizing the reporting process, we reduce administrative delays and help prioritize high-impact road repairs.
